Amanda Boutique

Welcome!

We opened in 2001 as a small gift shop. We added our piercing studio in 2003 after a formal apprenticeship. Due to an increase in popularity, we expanded to our second floor in 2009, where our piercings are done today.

History

We are currently a second generation family business. Our owner completed her formal apprenticeship for piercing in 2009. She is blood borne pathogen certified and First Aid and CPR certified and runs the shop with the help of her husband. They live in Manchester with their dog and three cats.
